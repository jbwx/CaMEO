CaMEO Index
Catalog of man-made extraterrestrial objects

This was a project made by Haim Hong and Jacob Westra, for the CalvinHacks Hackathon in March 2021.
This catalog includes all man-made objects outside of the Earth-Moon system. Non-probes like rocket boosters and heat shields are not included.
